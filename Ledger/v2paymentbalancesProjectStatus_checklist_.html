What’s new this week

  • 🎉 We've completed all the customer raised issues on PB alpha endpoints.[a][b]
  • ListActivities endpoint performance 30+ times faster on existing queries[c][d][e][f], and it allows any range query on the most busy merchant to have responses in at most ~1s 100 entries per page. Payment Balance ListActivities performance improvement
  • GetBalance endpoint has a 0 default balance when no activity has been persisted with updated_at value `null`.
  • Fixed incomplete fields on certain types of Activities
  • 🎉 We’ve also made further improvements to Payment Balance kafka feed in Q1
  • Decreased processing lag to within seconds when consuming Books to publish to PB kafka feed
  • Cleared dead letter queue by automatically backfilling the missing PB accounts[g][h][i][j] when consuming Books to publish PB activities
  • 🎉 Payment Balance Kafka feed has first prod customer-Payouts[k][l], to help them narrow down the scope of the merchants that need settlement
  • 🗓 Payment Balance Kafka feed will have the second prod customer - Cash for business in Q2. We will provide support for this customer and the work is a Q2 OKR.[m][n][o]
  • 🗓 Refund team are interested in calling PB GetBalance endpoint to replace previous Ledger’s GetPendingBalance endpoint[p][q]. This will help them get more accurate merchant balance in time.
  • ⚠️ `Complete high-level design for Payment Balances Read APIs Beta` deliverable will be punted to a future quarter after we’ve collected enough customer feedback[r][s][t][u]
  • ⚠️ As previously mentioned, most of our potential Alpha partners that had expressed interest in the API during discovery cannot test the API and provide feedback until Q3 or Q4 due to the tax season and migration from v1/settlements to v2/payouts. (Synder, a developer partner helping over 1,000 sellers, confirmed they will leverage the Payment Balances API, but will only start work with it in Q4 this year). Consequently, we plan to delay our Beta design until we receive their feedback[v].
